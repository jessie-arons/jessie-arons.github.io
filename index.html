I started playing hockey when I was 5 years old, and am now starting my 9th season with the Jr. Sharks girls program. I am 14 years old, going into 9th grade at Palo Alto High School. At the 2016 CAHA select camp, I was invited and attended the Multi-District camp in Colorado. My 2015-16 Jr Sharks team won the 14UAA Silver Stick regional tournament in Colorado, and was the first Jr. Sharks team to win the San Jose MLK Tourney in the 14UAAA division. I was an assistant captain on my 12UAAA team in 2014-15. I played three spring seasons with the Redwood City Black Star Boys, and was invited to practice with their Bantam A 2015-16 team. I also play the electric guitar at the School of Rock, am learning to code, and am interested in photography and web design. This summer I'm helping a non-profit create a library for children in Morocco. I love to play hockey and my goal is to play in college.
