I started playing hockey when I was 5 years old, and am now in my 10th season with the Jr. Sharks girls. I am 15 years old, in 10th grade at Palo Alto High School. This year I play on the Jr. Sharks Girls 16UAA (only 16s), and I'm the top goal scorer with 23 goals so far. I student coach the 8U Jr. Shark Girls. I was selected and attended the Multi-District camp in Colorado in 2016, and alternate in 2017-18. My 14UAAA team won Silver Stick Regional in CO, and was first Jr Sharks team to win 14UAAA MLK in San Jose. I was assistant captain of my 12UAAA team. I've also played with the Redwood City Black Star Boys.

Outside of hockey, I'm interested in photography and sign language and volunteer with the Morocco Library Project to create libraries for children.
